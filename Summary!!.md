🌍 AirAware

Predictive Air Intelligence & Citizen Climate Action Platform

Tagline:

From Air Quality Data → Community Climate Action

📌 Problem Statement

Urban air pollution and rising urban heat are major public health challenges.
Most existing applications only display AQI or weather data, but they do not help citizens:
understand their personal pollution exposure
avoid heat-stress zones
take meaningful actions to reduce pollution

Core Gap

Environmental awareness does not translate into actionable decisions or collective climate action.

💡 Solution

AirAware is an intelligent environmental platform that converts pollution and climate data into actionable insights for citizens and communities.

The platform:

* predicts short-term AQI trends
* detects urban heat-risk zones
* provides health alerts
* calculates personal pollution exposure
* suggests cleaner and cooler travel routes
* tracks community climate action

Goal:

Transform data → awareness → action → measurable environmental impact.

🚀 Key Features

* AQI Prediction
* Machine learning models forecast short-term air pollution trends using historical AQI and weather data.
* This allows users to anticipate pollution spikes before they occur.
* Pollution Risk Alerts
* Users receive notifications when pollution levels become unhealthy.

Examples:

* Avoid outdoor exercise
* Use protective masks
* Choose alternative travel routes
* Hyper-Local Pollution Map
* An interactive map visualizes pollution hotspots and cleaner zones within the city.
* Personal Pollution Exposure Score

AirAware estimates a user's daily pollution exposure based on:

* location
* travel routes
* time spent outdoors
* transportation mode

Example output:

Exposure Score: 72 / 100

Risk Level: High

Recommendation: Avoid outdoor travel during peak pollution hours.

Smart Clean Route Finder

AirAware suggests routes with lower pollution exposure, not just the shortest route.


Example:

Route A → 15 minutes (High Pollution)

Route B → 18 minutes (Cleaner Air)

HeatAware – Urban Heat Risk Detection

The platform also detects urban heat-stress zones using temperature and weather data.


Features include:

* heat-risk alerts
* cooler route suggestions
* heat exposure awareness

Example:

Heat Risk Level: High

Suggestion: Avoid outdoor travel between 12–3 PM.

Community Climate Leaderboard


Users earn Green Points for eco-friendly activities such as:
* cycling
* carpooling
* public transport
* walking
This encourages sustainable behavior through gamification.


⭐ Unique Feature:

Collective Action Impact Simulator

AirAware estimates how community behavior changes reduce pollution and emissions.


Example:

If 500 users carpool twice per week:

Fuel Saved: 320 Liters

CO₂ Reduced: 780 kg

This helps users visualize the impact of collective climate action.


📊 Community Climate Impact Dashboard:
